Solid Steel Radio Show


Boom Monk Ben & 2econd class citizen

Solid Steel in association with Serato (6th November) This week Boom Monk Ben has been busy in the lab and has just recorded a new mix for your listening pleasure. With the usual mixed bag of digital diversity, it takes in the UK Funky sounds of Roska, L-Vis 1990 and GreenMoney, the new skool bass of Malente, Diplo & Zinc, dubstep sounds from Joker, Rusko and Starkey as well as all the good bits in between. Headphones at the ready and don't delay....
2econd class citizen is up next, he has a new album 'A World Without' out now on Equinox records (one of Strictly Kev's album of the year). His mix features tracks from Jel, Yppah, the Slew and a few choice album tracks from the man himself, plus music from the psych/rock end of the spectrum with tracks from new york rock ensemble, Atilla and the mighty Power of Zeus.

DJ Cheeba - The Reels of Solid Steel



Using two turntables and Serato's Video-SL plug-in for Scratch Live to enable video controlled and manipulated by vinyl, Cheeba makes the most of this technology... mixing, scratching and juggling both sounds and pictures with stunning results. Original films, remixes and music videos made by Cheeba and James Reed (Thousand Thousand Points of Light) blend seamlessly with video mashups, animations and a wealth of other wonderful and often bizarre visual sources. With over 8 months producing the set and a further 6 developing it at shows, this revolutionary video mix has also been produced to coincide with the launch of www.djcheeba.com the new home of all Cheeba's mixtapes, videos, blog entries and much much more. Plug in, sit back and "Enjoy this trip... and it is a trip!"
